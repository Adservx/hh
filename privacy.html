Last Updated: November 6, 2025

Introduction

Prajkit, operated by Nebu DevOps (a sole proprietorship) ("we," "us"), is committed to protecting your privacy. This Policy outlines how we handle personal information when you use our website prajkit.com, app development services, or interact with us. By using our services, you agree to this Policy.

Information We Collect

  • Provided by You: Name, email, phone, billing details, project specs, and communications.
  • Automatically: IP address, device info, usage data via cookies (see Cookie Policy).
  • From Third Parties: Payment processors (e.g., Stripe).

Note: We do not collect sensitive data like health information.

How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver services and process payments

Sharing Information

We share your information only with:

We use encryption and security controls to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
