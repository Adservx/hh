Important Terms & General Notes

General Terms

  • Client may choose monthly or yearly payment (not both).
  • Yearly payment is cost-effective and preferred (often includes 1 month free).
  • Major redesigns, new modules, or feature development are not included in maintenance packages.
  • Hosting & domain renewal cost is separate unless specified.
  • Online payment gateways require valid business registration in Nepal.
  • Pricing may vary based on customization, content volume, and client feedback timeline.

Service Level Agreement (SLA)

  • SLA response time improves significantly with higher packages (Premium: 2-6 hours).
  • Maintenance covers existing system architecture, performance, and security.
  • Emergency or priority fixes are primarily included in Premium packages.
